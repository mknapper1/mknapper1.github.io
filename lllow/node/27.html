Low Associates Career Opportunities

We offer a competitive salary/benefit package including 401K, section 125, and opportunities for continuing education. Applicants should be responsible and possess a positive attitude with a willingness to learn.

 

  • Sales Associate
  • Computer Programmer/Analyst
  • Hardware Technician
  • Customer Software Support
  • Administrative Support Clerk

Sales Associate

Sorry, we currently have no openings for this position. Please continue to check our web site for more opportunities. If interested, you may still submit a resume. All resumes on file will be given consideration should an opening become available for the position requested.

Job Description

Establish and maintain activity on current and new customer accounts. Develop proposals to present to clients, conduct software demonstrations, and provide software follow-up as required.

Requirements

Associates degree in computer and/or business related field with a minimum of two years proven outside sales experience. Background experience in the education and/or government environments would be a plus. Applicants should be responsible and possess a positive attitude with a willingness to learn.

Contact
Please submit resume to:

employment@LLLow.com

Computer Programmer/Analyst

Job Description

Develop software based on the description of business logic. Work and produce the required results without close supervision.

Requirements

  • Experience desired in one or more of the following languages: Delphi, C++, Microsoft Visual Studio.
  • Object oriented programming
  • Relational databases
  • Web development background
  • Thorough knowledge of business processes; have problem-solving abilities; be detail and deadline-oriented; and be organized and collaborative.

Contact
Please submit resume to:

employment@LLLow.com
