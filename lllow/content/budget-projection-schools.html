Low Associates budget projection software

Since 1975, Low Associates has provided the most comprehensive software available for the State of Indiana. Our quality and support are unmatched!

Budget Projection Software Highlights:

  • published & adopted budget uploads to gateway 
  • allow departments to enter their own budget requests remotely
  • stores budget history for reporting purposes
  • customizable reporting, allows you to compare current & historical budgets
  • project for the future based off of a percentage or flat dollar amount
  • Interfaces with our financial software & update approved budgets into financial software   
  • import previous year's expenditures, compare appropriated versus expended
  • export in multiple formats
  • customizable grids & views
  • combined individual & group security
  • on screen help files

Our Software Integrates With:

Accountability

Accountability interfaces with the Low Associates Payroll/Personnel/Financial Software, which allows customers to process their W-2’s, 1099’s and their ACA information.
American Fidelity

American Fidelity interfaces with the Low Associates Payroll Software after customers sign up during open enrollment with American Fidelity. 
Doculivery

Doculivery interfaces with the Low Associates Payroll Software by providing their employees with online access to Check Stubs and W2’s.
Right Stuff Software

Right Stuff interfaces with the Low Associates Payroll software by allowing time and attendance to be exported and imported each pay day.
AESOP

AESOP interfaces with the Low Associates Payroll software by allowing time and attendance to be exported and imported each pay day for substitutes.

Advanced Time Management

Advanced Time Management interfaces with the Low Associates Payroll software by allowing time and attendance to be exported and imported each pay day.
Applitrack

Applitrack interfaces with the Low Associates Personnel Software by allowing customers to import information about job applicants and new hires.
